How to play

  • Orders (beans) arrive across 3 rollups (lanes).
  • Process orders in the global arrival order.
  • Next Required ID: 1
  • Press A / S / D to serve from Lane 1 / 2 / 3.
  • Space: Activate Shared Sequencer (auto-correct for 5s).
  • Private orders (🔒) are safe to serve anytime during power-up.
  • Bribes (💰) tempt you to go out-of-order. Do it and Risk ↑.
  • Score = Throughput × Fairness bonus. Keep Risk low!
